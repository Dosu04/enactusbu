Who we are!

Enactus is the world’s largest experiential learning platform dedicated to creating a better world while developing the next generation of entrepreneurial leaders and social innovators.
Since its inception in Nigeria, the Enactus programme has grown from one campus in 2001 to over 30 campuses spread across 25 states of the federation with an annual average of 1500 active student members.
